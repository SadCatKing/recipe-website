Ingredients

  • Boiled eggs
  • Mayo
  • Paprika

How many eggs? just follow your heart. but in all honestly if you're planing on serving guests during the holidays or other events; a whole carton of 24 eggs (it will give you a chance to serve seconds) for yourself idk 12 carton of eggs

Google if you don't know how to boiled eggs, and make sure its hard boiled.

Avoid the "sweeter" kind of mayo like Miracle Whip, its just not that pleasant of a taste to have in deviled eggs what I usually go for is the Hellman mayo, its more savory which is what we want in this recipes

Paprika is always optional but it adds a wonderful smokey flavor to the eggs

Steps

  1. Cut your boiled eggs in half
  2. put the yolks into a bowl, and put the white of the eggs on your serving plate.
  3. crush all the yolks with whatever utensil you have
  4. add a tablespoon of mayo at the time to the yolks, with each new tablesppon due a taste test to see if its you your liking
  5. Once the filing is up to your standard, grab a spoon and put back the yolks onto the whites of the eggs
  6. remember their should be enough of the yolks for all of yours eggs so spread evenly
  7. sprinkle paprika onto the eggs
  8. put a plastic wrap over the eggs or a lid (make sure their sealed correctly if not leaving the devil eggs to the elements will make them actually fucking unedible
  9. let them chill in the fridge for a bit or if like me just eat them right away.
